Why Is My AC Leaking Water Inside?

A leaking AC isn't just a mechanical problemâ€”it's a threat to your home's flooring and drywall. Here's why the water is staying inside instead of heading out.

Clogged Condensate Drain Line

Over time, algae and debris can plug the PVC pipe that carries condensation away. When this happens, the water backs up into the indoor unit's drain pan and overflows.

Frozen Evaporator Coils

If your AC is low on refrigerant or has a dirty filter, the coils can freeze into a block of ice. When that ice melts, it produces more water than the drain pan can handle.

Stop the Damage Now

Water damage spreads fast and leads to mold growth. Don't wait for a flood.
