Why Is My Furnace Blowing Cold?

If your vents are blowing cold air, it's usually one of four things:

  • Thermostat Settings: Ensure it's set to 'Auto', not 'On'.
  • Overheating: A dirty filter can cause the limit switch to trip.
  • Pilot/Igniter Failure: The most common mechanical failure in Lebanon homes.
  • Condensate Line Clog: Especially common in high-efficiency furnaces.
