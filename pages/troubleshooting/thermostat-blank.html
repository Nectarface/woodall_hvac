System Power Issues

Thermostat Screen Blank? Power Issues Explained.

When your thermostat goes dark, your entire HVAC system goes with it. Before you assume the worst, check these power sources.

The Batteries

Many thermostats rely on AA or AAA batteries. Even if you have a 'C-wire', batteries are often used for backup. Replace them with fresh ones and see if the screen returns.

The Breaker Box

HVAC systems pull a lot of power. A surge could have tripped the breaker. Check your electrical panel for any switches that aren't fully in the 'ON' position.

The Safety Switch

If your AC drain line is clogged, a 'float switch' may have triggered, cutting power to the system to prevent a flood. This will often result in a blank thermostat.

Professional Warning

Electrical issues can be dangerous and lead to system board failure. If a breaker keeps tripping, let a licensed tech handle it.
